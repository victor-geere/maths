# Tensor Products

The **tensor product** $M \otimes_R N$ of two $R$-modules is the universal target for $R$-bilinear maps out of $M \times N$. It generalises the tensor product of vector spaces (when $R$ is a field) and is the key construction encoding **base change** (scalar extension), the monoidal structure on $R$-modules, and the derived functor $\mathrm{Tor}$. Concretely, $M \otimes_R N$ is generated by symbols $m \otimes n$ (for $m \in M$, $n \in N$), subject to bilinearity over $R$: $(m_1 + m_2) \otimes n = m_1 \otimes n + m_2 \otimes n$, $m \otimes (n_1 + n_2) = m \otimes n_1 + m \otimes n_2$, and $mr \otimes n = m \otimes rn$.

## Construction

$$M \otimes_R N = \frac{\text{Free abelian group on } M \times N}{\text{bilinearity relations over }R}$$

The class of $(m,n)$ is written $m \otimes n$. Key relations:
- $(m_1 + m_2) \otimes n = m_1 \otimes n + m_2 \otimes n$
- $m \otimes (n_1 + n_2) = m \otimes n_1 + m \otimes n_2$
- $mr \otimes n = m \otimes rn$ (for $r \in R$)

## Universal Property

For any abelian group $A$ and any $R$-bilinear map $f: M \times N \to A$, there is a unique group homomorphism $\tilde{f}: M \otimes_R N \to A$ with $\tilde{f}(m \otimes n) = f(m,n)$.

## Key Properties

- **Right exactness**: $M \otimes_R -$ is right-exact; $- \otimes_R N$ is right-exact.
- **Flat modules**: $M$ is flat $\iff$ $M \otimes_R -$ is exact.
- $R \otimes_R M \cong M$ (unit)
- $(M \oplus M') \otimes_R N \cong (M \otimes_R N) \oplus (M' \otimes_R N)$ (distributivity)
- $R/I \otimes_R M \cong M/IM$ (important special case)
- $S \otimes_R M$ is the **base change** of $M$ along $R \to S$.

## Glossary

| Term / Symbol | Meaning |
|---|---|
| $M \otimes_R N$ | tensor product of $R$-modules $M$ and $N$ |
| $m \otimes n$ | elementary tensor: image of $(m,n)$ |
| Bilinear map | $f: M \times N \to A$ linear in each variable over $R$ |
| Universal property | $M \otimes_R N$ is initial target for $R$-bilinear maps |
| Right-exact functor | preserves exactness of $0 \to M \to N \to P$ at $N$ and $P$ |
| Flat module | $M \otimes_R -$ is exact |
| Base change $S \otimes_R M$ | extension of scalars from $R$ to $S$ |
| $R/I \otimes_R M \cong M/IM$ | reduction modulo $I$ |
| $\mathrm{Tor}_i^R(M,N)$ | derived functors of $\otimes_R$; measure failure of flatness |
